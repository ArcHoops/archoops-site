Teaching kids data through predicting basketball game outcomes.

ArcHoops is an interactive web application that teaches data science through basketball analytics.

Our Product

Currently in development, our platform combines education, gaming, and sports to create an engaging learning experience.

Learn

Students explore basketball analytics and data-driven decision-making through interactive video lessons delivered by their favorite players.

Practice

They apply what they've learned by predicting key metrics for upcoming NBA games using historical data.

Compare

Students earn points based on prediction accuracy, compare with friends and classmates, and compete to improve stats.

Vision

Empowering the next generation of diverse STEM leaders through sports analytics.

Mission

Transforming kids' enthusiasm for sports into a powerful tool for learning about data science.

Business Description

ArcHoops addresses the critical lack of diversity in STEM and sports analytics through an engaging educational platform that makes data science accessible and exciting for middle school students.

The Problem

  • Only 9% of the STEM workforce in the US is Black or African American
  • Fewer than 5% of NBA analytics professionals are African American, compared to 70% of players
  • Traditional STEM programs often fail to connect with students' interests

Target Audience

  • Middle school students (grades 6-8)
  • Schools and educational districts
  • After-school sports clubs and youth programs
  • Parents seeking engaging STEM education
